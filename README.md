# Hi, I'm Taylor

I’m a programmer sometimes, future educator, and advocate for the Indie Web.

I have a BS in Software Engineering and I am working on my MA ing secondary education – english. My goal for my career is to teach computer science and english in highschools. I know, weird combo, but I firmly belive they have a lot in common and actully build off eachother very well.

## About Me

I am someone who sees programming as a creative outlet. I strongly believe in the indie web philosophy that individuals should be able to create their own personal spaces online, where they have complete ownership and the freedom to design and customize their own experiences. I don't like algorithm based social media, and I believe it has taken away from our creativity online. I have professional development experience, but working in the industry killed the vibe for me. Now, I only work on personal projects, open-source projects, and tools for other people on the indie web.
